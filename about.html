Project Pandora

We are not a garage start-up that go super lucky in building the best technology. We are an army of engineers full of creativity and innovation. We strive to give our customers what they truly deserve, the best.

We're located at the heart of Seattle, WA; the Sea City. A city full of skilled professionals and passionate students that keep us inspired to give our best in everything we do. Our products are designed with the environment and hard-working people in mind. The primary map of every idea has the global goals set by the United Nations, incorporated right into their design process.

Mouli Yadav, Co-Founder & CTO of Project Pandora, is an environmentalist. He makes sure everything spent and produced is eco-friendly. Vidhan Reddy, Co-founder & CMO, is an MIT graduate that makes sure our quality of work is always at its best and often brings in new innovations. Lastly, Charan Manikanta, Founder & CEO of Project Pandora. He is a man of true vision and started the Project Pandora, a revolution to build for a better world.

Project Pandora is the vision of these three people, born out of their zeal to build a sustainable & an inclusive future.

We are commited towards doing our best for the Global Goals set by the United Nations
